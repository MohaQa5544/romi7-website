fix(admin): bullet-proof modal scroll using overflow on overlay

The previous attempt put max-h+overflow on the card, but with
items-center the card was vertically centered and could spill
above the viewport (top of dialog cut off, no way to reach it).

Switched to the canonical pattern: outer overlay has overflow-y-auto,
inner wrapper is min-h-full + flex centered, card is normal-height.
When card is taller than viewport, the overlay itself scrolls and
the user can reach every part of the card. Applied to all four
admin dialogs.
